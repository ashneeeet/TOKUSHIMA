HISTORY

Tokushima, located on Shikoku Island, Japan, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It was a significant center during the Edo period, famous for its Awa Odori dance festival. Historically, it was ruled by the Tokushima Domain under the Tokugawa shogunate. Today, it's renowned for its natural beauty and festivals.
